ENID – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

ENID is a senior-friendly Android launcher app. Contact: support.enid@gmail.com

2. What data we store

ENID stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Your name (to display a personalised greeting)
  • Names and phone numbers of saved contacts
  • Emergency contacts
  • App settings (e.g. auto-call 112)
  • Subscription status and active plan (managed by Google Play)

None of this data is transmitted to our servers. Everything stays on your phone.

3. Permissions we request

  • Phone: to make and detect calls
  • Contacts: to import phone contacts during setup
  • SMS: to display and receive incoming messages
  • Notifications: to detect WhatsApp messages and calls
  • Location: required for emergency calls

You can revoke any permission at any time in Android Settings → Apps → ENID → Permissions.

5. Third-party services

  • Google Play Billing: subscription management
  • Firebase Crashlytics: anonymous crash reports to improve the app
  • WhatsApp: ENID reads call notifications only — message content is never stored

6. Data security

All data is stored locally on your device. It is protected by Android's app sandboxing — other apps cannot access it.

7. Children

ENID is not directed at children under 16.

8. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have the right to access or delete your data. To delete: Android Settings → Apps → ENID → Storage → Clear Data. For any privacy request: support.enid@gmail.com

9. Contact
